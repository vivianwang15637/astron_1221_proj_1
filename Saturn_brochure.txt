
=== Saturn Space Tour ===
Travel to Saturn Base Cost: $1007.0	Distance:10.07 AU
Gas giant with a prominent ring system made of ice and dust and massive gaseous atmosphere

Activity 1: Saturn's Ring Hopping Adventure

Cost: $299.5
Take a family-friendly shuttle tour through Saturn's magnificent ring system, where you'll witness ice particles and rocky debris up close while learning about the planet's formation. Kids receive special junior astronaut certificates and ring-themed souvenirs to commemorate this once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Activity 2: Titan Moon Base Family Camp

Cost: $425.75
Spend two days at a cozy family lodge on Saturn's largest moon Titan, complete with methane lake kayaking and hydrocarbon waterfall hikes suitable for all ages. The package includes all meals, educational workshops about alien geology, and evening stargazing sessions with Saturn's rings as your backdrop.

Activity 3: Enceladus Ice Geyser Safari

Cost: $281.75
Board a specially designed observation pod to safely witness the spectacular ice geysers erupting from Saturn's moon Enceladus, shooting crystalline plumes hundreds of miles into space. This thrilling 4-hour excursion includes hot cocoa service and interactive exhibits about the possibility of life in Enceladus's subsurface ocean.
